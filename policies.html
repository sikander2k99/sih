Advanced Policies

Below you'll find a comprehensive overview of the policies that govern our fitness center. We prioritize transparency, safety, and member satisfaction.

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. All personal information collected is kept confidential and is used solely for membership and service purposes. We will never share or sell your data to third parties without your consent.

For more details, refer to our full privacy policy.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Members can cancel their membership at any time with 7 days’ notice. Refunds for unused sessions are available upon request, provided that the sessions are canceled within 30 days of the purchase date. Members on annual plans are eligible for prorated refunds based on usage.

Membership Freezing Policy

Members can pause their membership for a maximum of 3 months each year. To freeze your membership, you must notify us at least 14 days in advance.

Liability Waiver

By using our facilities, you acknowledge that engaging in physical activities can involve risks. You agree to release us from any liability for injuries that may occur while using our equipment or participating in classes. It’s important to consult with a healthcare professional before starting any fitness program.

Code of Conduct

We expect all members to act respectfully toward staff and other members. Discrimination, harassment, and inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated. Violations may result in suspension or cancellation of membership without refund.

Equipment & Facility Usage

Members are required to follow proper etiquette when using gym equipment, including cleaning machines after use and returning weights to their designated places. Damage caused by improper use of equipment will be the responsibility of the member.

Payment Policy

All payments are due in advance on a monthly or annual basis. We accept credit cards, debit cards, and direct transfers. Late payments may result in suspension of access to the gym and its services.
